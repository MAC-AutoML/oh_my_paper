# Paper AI workflow map

Use this map to route one phase at a time:

1. Research process: idea/results become brief, claims, evidence map, experiment plan.
2. Writing: section drafts are built from claim IDs and evidence status.
3. Figures: visuals are planned around one takeaway and claim support.
4. Layout: page budget and template assumptions are checked.
5. Reviewer: strict review separates fatal, major, minor, and wording issues.
6. Rebuttal: review concerns become evidence-backed responses and promised revisions.
7. Eval loop: weak outputs become fixtures under `tests/fixtures/evals/`.

Never route directly from vague idea to final paper. Require artifacts and gates.
