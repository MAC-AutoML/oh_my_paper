# Toy Paper Brief

## Working title

Evidence-Gated Toy Agent for Table Reasoning

## Problem

Small research teams need a safe way to draft paper claims without turning missing experiment ideas into overstated results.

## Proposed idea

A toy agent keeps a claim ledger and refuses to strengthen unsupported claims during drafting.

## Target audience

Researchers evaluating workflow tooling for academic writing.

## Current evidence

- Synthetic benchmark setup exists.
- One pilot result suggests claim-ledger prompts reduce unsupported claims.
- Robustness claim is not yet supported.
